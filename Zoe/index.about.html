About Me

Sculpting & Crafting

I am a specialist in life-casting and sculpture. I opened my shop Zoe's Crafting and Sculpting 7 years ago and since then I enjoy creating amazing family memories for my clients. I truly understand how precious these tangible memories are for our families,so I am always happy to offer technical tips and advice as well as a unique professional tidy & paint service and options to make casting of forever holding hands in my own study.
